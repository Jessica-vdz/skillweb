Why is poetry so important to me?

Somethimes when i get overwelmd or when i get frustated i don't know what to do or what to say. Writing is an escape for me. It's an escape from my head. Because sometimes i feel like i'm inside an empty white room without doors and i keep walking in circles without a wayout. Poetry is my way out of the white empty room

Whitney Hanson

Whitney Hanson is the author of Home and Climate. Through Whitney's vulnerability and authenticity, she has connected with thousands of readers and adamantly believes that poetry is not a dead language; rather it is the key to unlocking true vulnerability which leads to deeper connection with one another.

Rupi Kaur

Rupi Kaur is a Canadian poet, illustrator, photographer, and author. Born in Punjab, India, Kaur immigrated to Canada at a young age with her family. She began performing poetry in 2009 and rose to fame on Instagram, eventually becoming a popular poet through her three collections of poetry
